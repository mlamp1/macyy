About Me

I grew up in San Diego, California, and have been lucky enough to have always been surrounded by art and unique designs. My love for design and creativity began early, and I expressed it through music, writing, film, and fashion.

I found my love for advertising in college through an Intro to Advertising class, and have never looked back! With a major in Marketing and a minor in Design, I cannot wait to share my love for beautiful brands with others.
